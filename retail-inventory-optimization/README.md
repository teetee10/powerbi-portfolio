# Retail Inventory Optimization & Demand Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance, inventory efficiency, and stock risk using Power BI.  
The dashboard is designed to support **executive decision-making** and **operational planning**.
---
## Business Problem
Retail teams lacked visibility into how inventory levels aligned with customer demand, resulting in:
- Excess inventory tied up in slow-moving products
- Risk of stockouts for high-demand items
- Limited prioritization of high-value products
---
## Objectives
- Monitor sales and demand trends
- Evaluate inventory efficiency
- Identify high-impact products using ABC (Pareto) analysis
- Detect stockout risk early
- Enable product-level drill-through analysis
---
## Data Model
- Fact_InventorySales
- Dim_Product
- Dim_Date
- Dim_Store

Best practices applied:
- Star schema design
- Single-direction relationships
- Hidden surrogate keys
- Centralized measures table
---
## Key Analytics
- Sales & demand trends (MoM / YoY)
- Inventory turnover & days of coverage
- Stockout risk indicators
- Pareto (ABC) product classification
- Drill-through product analysis
---
## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Inventory Overview
2. Sales, Demand & Region
3. Inventory Risk & Action
4. Time Intelligence
5. Pareto (ABC)
6. Product Categories
7. Product Drill-Through
---
## Business Impact
This solution enables organizations to:
- Reduce excess inventory
- Prevent revenue loss from stockouts
- Focus on high-value products
- Improve inventory planning decisions
